Our Specialized Treatments

Comprehensive care tailored to your recovery needs.

Musculoskeletal Physio

Expert care for muscles, joints, and bones. Ideal for back pain, neck pain, and arthritis management.

Neurorehabilitation

Specialized therapy for stroke, paralysis, and nerve injuries to restore motor function and independence.

Sports Rehab

Fast-track recovery for athletes suffering from sprains, ligament tears, and muscle injuries.

Paediatric Physio

Gentle, play-based therapy for children with developmental delays, cerebral palsy, and congenital conditions.

Pre & Post Surgical Rehab

Preparation before surgery and guided recovery after operations like knee or hip replacements.

Pre & Post Partum Rehab

Specialized care for women during and after pregnancy to manage back pain and core strength.

Pilates

Core strengthening exercises to improve posture, flexibility, and overall body balance.

Cupping Therapy

Ancient technique to improve blood flow, relieve muscle tension, and promote relaxation.

Dry Needling

Targeted therapy using thin needles to release muscle knots (trigger points) and reduce chronic pain.

Kinesio Taping

Supportive taping technique to stabilize joints and muscles without restricting movement.

Chiropractic & Osteopathy

Manual adjustments to align the spine and skeletal system for better health and pain relief.

IASTM

Instrument-Assisted Soft Tissue Mobilization to break down scar tissue and improve mobility.
